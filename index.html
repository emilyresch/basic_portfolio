About Emily

Hey there. My name is Emily and I'm an aspiring web developer. I earned my Bachelor's Degree in 2017 for Animation and Illustration from DePaul University. In the fall of 2019, I decided to return to school to pursue a Master's Degree in Human-Computer interaction. After taking a few intro coding courses for the degree, I found that I truly enjoyed coding more than anything I had done before in school. I have put my degree on pause (hopefully forever!) to take the coding bootcamp course that Northwestern offers to up my employment game and jumpstart a career in development.
I have had the opportunity to live abroad twice with my family and travel a good chunk of the globe with them (and through some study-abroad programs). My favorite place I have travelled to so far would have to be Tokyo, Japan or South Africa. Thanks to my mom's side of the family, I am also fluent in French.
In my free time, I like to draw digitally, watch The Office (for the hundredth time), listen to music, brew beer with my dad, drink beer with my dad, and cook new recipes I find on the internet.
